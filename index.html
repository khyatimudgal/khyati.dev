✦ About Me

Hey, I'm Khyati Mudgal — an 18-year-old self-taught developer from Uttarakhand, India, building my career one project at a time.

I started coding in April 2026 with nothing but curiosity and a blank browser tab — and within months had real projects live on the internet. No bootcamp, no degree, no shortcuts. Just consistency, late nights, and a genuine love for building things. I learn fast, I build faster, and I'm always onto the next thing. Right now I'm deep into React, TypeScript, and everything it takes to become a full-stack developer worth hiring.

Beyond the code, I'm a creator at heart. I paint, draw, make playlists, and pick up languages just for the joy of it. I care deeply about making things that are both functional and beautiful — big on aesthetics, bigger on actually shipping. I believe the best developers are creative thinkers first — people who see problems differently and bring something more than just technical knowledge to the table. That's the kind of developer I'm becoming.

I'm ambitious, I'm self-aware, and I care deeply about the work I put out into the world. I'm currently working towards my first remote role while building things I'm genuinely proud of — and this is just the beginning.

Glad you're here. 🖤
